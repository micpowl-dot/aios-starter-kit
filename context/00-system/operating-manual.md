# Layer 00 — System (the operating brain)

The always-on layer. This is HOW you operate, independent of any project. It loads first, every
session. Keep it short and stable.

The canonical operating manual is the root **`CLAUDE.md`** (filled by `/onboard`). This file is the
system-layer index; point your agent here and to the framework reference.

## What belongs in the system layer
- **The Three Ms and Four Cs** — `references/3ms-4cs-framework.md`. The brain rewire and the build order.
- **Voice and output rules** — direct, bullets over paragraphs, short sentences, no em dashes;
  external-facing content is a draft until approved. (Filled with your real voice in `references/voice.md`.)
- **Standing behaviors** — log meaningful decisions to `decisions/log.md`; before doing a task
  manually, ask "to what extent can AI leverage this?"; surface anything seen 3+ times as automation bait.
- **The weekly ritual** — `/audit` finds the gap, `/level-up` ships one artifact. One per week compounds.

## What does NOT belong here
Project specifics (those are layer 01), or one-off task instructions (layer 02). If it changes when
you switch projects, it is not system-layer.

> Edit this layer quarterly, after a `/level-up` Q90 review, or when your role or voice changes.
